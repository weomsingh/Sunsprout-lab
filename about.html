About Sunsprout Labs

The thinking behind the brand.

Sunsprout Labs was created to build a more intelligent approach to everyday nutrition. We believe snacks should be crafted like research-driven products: studied, tested, refined, and trusted.

Why we started Notes

Conventional snacks often prioritize shelf appeal over substance. We saw an opportunity to rethink roasting and flavour development as a controlled process, not a shortcut.

  • Address gaps in ingredient transparency.
  • Replace oil-heavy methods with precision roasting.
  • Build a lab-minded standard for consistency.

To redefine snacking through research, care, and intelligent food design.
